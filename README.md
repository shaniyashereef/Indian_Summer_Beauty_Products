# 🌞 Indian Summer Beauty Products Analysis

## 📌 Overview
This project analyzes beauty and skincare product trends during Indian summers. The focus is on understanding consumer preferences, product effectiveness, and seasonal demand patterns.

---

## 🎯 Objective
- To analyze popular beauty products used in summer
- To study trends based on skin type and product category
- To identify key factors influencing product choice
- To provide insights for better product recommendations

---

## 🛠️ Tools & Technologies
- Excel (Data Cleaning & Analysis)
- Power BI  (Visualization)

---

## 📊 Dataset
The dataset includes:
- Product names and categories (sunscreen, face wash, moisturizer, etc.)
- Price range
- Skin type suitability
- Customer ratings and reviews
- Usage trends during summer

*(Dataset sourced from public platforms or manually created for analysis)*

---

## 📈 Key Insights
- Sunscreens and lightweight moisturizers are highly preferred during summer  
- Products suitable for oily and combination skin show higher demand  
- Higher-rated products tend to have better ingredient transparency  
- Affordable products dominate the market in India  

---

## 📉 Visualizations
- Product category distribution  
- Price vs rating analysis  
- Skin type vs product preference  
- Seasonal demand trends  


---

## ✅ Conclusion
Indian consumers prefer affordable, lightweight, and skin-friendly products during summer. Brands focusing on sunscreen, hydration, and oil control have higher demand.
